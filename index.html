Privacy Policy

DraftSafe

Last updated: January 2025  |  Chrome Extension

DraftSafe does not collect, transmit, or share any of your data. Everything stays on your device.

What We Collect

DraftSafe does not collect any personal information. The extension saves text content locally on your device only, using Chrome's built-in storage API. This data never leaves your browser.

How Your Data Is Stored

All draft content is stored using chrome.storage.local, which means it exists only on your computer. We have no access to it. No one does except you.

Password Fields

DraftSafe is designed to completely skip any field marked as a password input. It never reads, saves, or interacts with passwords under any circumstance.

Third Parties

DraftSafe does not use any analytics tools, advertising services, or third-party SDKs. There are no trackers of any kind inside the extension.

Data Deletion

You can delete individual saved drafts or clear all data at any time directly from the extension popup. Uninstalling the extension removes all locally stored data automatically.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the new version will be published at this URL with a revised date at the top.
